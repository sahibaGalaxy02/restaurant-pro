About Us

Golden Spoon has been serving exquisite dishes since 1995. Our chefs blend tradition and innovation, bringing flavors from around the world to your plate.

At Golden Spoon, we believe that dining is more than just eating — it’s an experience. Since our opening, we’ve been dedicated to bringing people together over delicious food, elegant ambiance, and heartfelt hospitality. Our chefs carefully craft each dish using the freshest ingredients, blending traditional recipes with modern creativity. From our cozy interiors bathed in warm golden tones to the inviting aroma from our kitchen, every detail is designed to make you feel at home. Whether you’re joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, Golden Spoon promises an unforgettable culinary journey — one bite at a time.
